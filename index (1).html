Who is The Chameleon?

I am the type of person who unconcisouly mimics postures, mannerisms, facial expressions, and other's behaviors and actions. I also sometimes unintentionally changes my own actions actions to match that of of others in ones current social environment. But I do not necessarily mean to do this, but it is all in the actions of my unconscious. I would say I am a very special person, who is one of a kind. It is rare to be a Chameleon in this day and age, where everyone strives to be different from one another. But not for me, nope. I just want to be like everyone else around me.

I AM THE CHAMELEON!

Whether I am content with myself or not, I tend to model the actions of society around me without even thinking about it. Maybe I am not happy with myself and who I am as a person. I do not know. I first received the name Chameleon when I was in grade school. I was initially given this nickname when my friends started realizing that I was wearing what they were wearing, saying what they were saying, and acting how they were acting. Some would take that as a compliment, me doing exactly as they were, but instead they named me the Chameleon.

Was I happy with that?

Truth is, it did not matter to me. As the Chameleon I acted exactly as my peers around me. They all started calling me the Chameleon, so what did I do? I started calling all of them the Chameleon too! What can I say, I am more myself when I am directly imitating the actions of others around me. I do not need my own identity. I want to be like everyone else around me so I fit in and so I feel like I belong.
